Ireland Population

This page contains data from the census unit at the CSO and the Northern Ireland Research and Statistics Agency (NISRA). This data was developed under the Evidence Based Planning theme of the Ireland Northern Cross Border Cooperation Observatory (INICCO-2) and CrosSPlaN-2 funded research programme. The web page includes data on Population, Gender and Age-groups within the County Areas in the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland. For more information on the original data source please see AIRO, CSO and NISRA
